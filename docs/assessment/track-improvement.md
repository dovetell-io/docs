---
title: Track improvement
description: Use repeated assessments to see whether your team's AI practices are becoming more reliable.
---

The assessment is most useful when a team retakes it over time.

The first result gives you a baseline. Later results show whether your team is
moving from scattered AI usage toward more structured, coordinated, and
repeatable work.

## What to compare

Compare:

- total score
- maturity level
- strongest capability areas
- weakest capability areas
- recommended next steps
- changes after adopting templates or workflow changes

## When to retake it

Retake the assessment after a meaningful change, such as:

- adopting a shared context structure
- adding checkpoint and closeout habits
- using a new template package
- changing how AI-assisted work is reviewed
- onboarding a new team or project

## How to use the result

Use the result to choose the next smallest useful improvement.

The goal is not to chase a score. The goal is to make team context easier to
find, reuse, review, and improve.
