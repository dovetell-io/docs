---
title: How the assessment works
description: The dovetell assessment produces a score, recommendations, and a durable link for tracking progress over time.
---

The assessment is designed to take about five minutes.

It asks 30 questions, scores the team's current practices, and gives the team a
durable link they can use to track progress over time.

## Flow

1. Take the assessment.
2. Enter an email address.
3. Get maturity results.
4. Save the unique project link.
5. Retake later to track progress.

## Inputs

Required:

- email address
- assessment answers

Optional:

- project name
- role
- team size
- company
- industry
- primary AI tool

## Output

The assessment returns:

- maturity level
- score out of 90
- section breakdown
- strongest areas
- biggest gaps
- recommended next steps

Those next steps can point to free docs, templates, setup review, or later
platform capabilities.

## Entry point logic

The assessment should route users to the lightest useful next action.

Examples:

- A scattered team may need a free checklist or starter context template.
- A structured team may need reusable prompts or handoff routines.
- A coordinated team may need review governance and drift monitoring.
- A compounding team may be ready for maintained templates or platform support.

The assessment should not push every team toward the same product. That would
be noisy. The value is matching the recommendation to the team's current
operating maturity.
